ProblemSet Problem - L Euler Function

Given $w$ as a prime,

  1. we have $\phi\left(a * w\right) = \phi\left(a\right) * w$ when $w$ is a fator of $a$;
  2. otherwise, we have $\phi\left(a * w\right) = \left(a\right) * \left(w - 1\right)$.
